What is YMLA?

  • YMLA (Yeast Multiple List Analyzer) aims to help analysis multiple gene lists in yeast

  • YMLA includes the most comprehensive features to date (23 datasets, 39 features) in yeast to facilitate analysis of the feature enrichment among multiple gene lists

  • A customized feature comparison function is provided in YMLA to assist mechanism hypotheses
